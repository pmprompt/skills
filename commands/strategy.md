# /strategy — Product Strategy Workflow

Complete product strategy from positioning to defensibility.

## What It Does

Chains positioning, competitive analysis, and strategic narrative to create a coherent product strategy.

## When to Use

- New product or market entry
- Repositioning existing product
- Need strategic clarity for roadmap decisions
- Stakeholder alignment on direction

## Workflow

1. **Positioning Canvas** — Define competitive alternatives, differentiated value, target customers, and category (uses `positioning-canvas` skill)

2. **7 Powers Analysis** — Identify sources of durable competitive advantage (uses `seven-powers` skill)

3. **Strategic Narrative** — Craft the "why now" story connecting market forces to your strategy (uses `strategic-narrative` skill)

## Usage

```
/strategy [product or market]
```

**Example:**
```
/strategy B2B project management tool for remote agencies
```

## Output

- Completed Positioning Canvas
- 7 Powers assessment with top 2-3 power sources
- Strategic narrative (5-part story)
- Recommended focus areas

## Next Steps After /strategy

- `/discover` — Validate opportunities with customers
- `/write-prd` — Document first feature set
- `/pricing` — Design monetization strategy
