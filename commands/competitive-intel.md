# /competitive-intel — Competitive Intelligence Workflow

Deep competitive analysis from positioning to strategic moats.

## What It Does

Chains competitive analysis, power dynamics, and positioning to understand your competitive landscape and identify differentiation opportunities.

## When to Use

- Entering new market or category
- Repositioning against competitors
- Strategic planning cycle
- Investor or board presentation prep

## Workflow

1. **Competitive Analysis** — Map competitors' strengths, weaknesses, and positioning (uses `competitive-analysis-framework` skill)

2. **7 Powers** — Identify competitors' power sources and your opportunities (uses `seven-powers` skill)

3. **Positioning Canvas** — Clarify your unique differentiation (uses `positioning-canvas` skill)

## Usage

```
/competitive-intel [market or category]
```

**Example:**
```
/competitive-intel Design tool market (Figma, Sketch, Adobe XD)
```

## Output

- Competitive landscape map
- Competitor strengths/weaknesses analysis
- 7 Powers assessment (who has which powers)
- Recommended positioning and differentiation strategy

## Next Steps After /competitive-intel

- `/strategy` — Build full strategic narrative
- `/pricing` — Competitive pricing analysis
- Update positioning on website and sales materials
