# Competitive Analysis Framework

## When to Use
- Launching a new product or entering a market
- Planning your product positioning and differentiation
- Investors or stakeholders asking about competition
- Identifying feature gaps and opportunities
- Understanding competitive threats and advantages

## What This Skill Does
Creates comprehensive competitive analysis covering features, positioning, strengths, weaknesses, and identifies differentiation opportunities.

   - UX/UI approach and design philosophy
   - Go-to-market strategy
